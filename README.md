# Voice-Call-Social-Platform-Database

Mission Statement:
To design and implement a robust relational database for a real-time voice-call social platform that efficiently manages user identities, room lifecycles, and session participation. The system aims to provide a scalable foundation for real-time audio interaction, content moderation, and financial transaction auditing while ensuring data integrity and reporting capabilities.

Mission Objectives
1. Data Maintenance (Enter, Update, and Delete)
To maintain data on users and their comprehensive profiles.
To maintain data on user account statuses and hierarchical roles.
To maintain data on diverse room types (Public/Private) and their settings.
To maintain data on room ownership and the creation-to-closure lifecycle.
To maintain data on call session logs and connectivity status.
To maintain data on participant interaction roles and real-time states.
To maintain data on moderation actions and administrator IDs.
To maintain data on user wallets and transaction histories.
To maintain data on virtual economy fees and premium features.
To maintain data on system events and notification logs.
2. Data Searching & Retrieval
To perform searches on user profiles and account statuses.
To perform searches on available rooms by types, limits, or tags.
To perform searches on call session telemetry and timestamps.
To perform searches on participant join/leave times and metrics.
To perform searches on moderation history and safety audit trails.
To perform searches on financial transactions and billing records.
To perform searches on invitation alerts and missed call notifications.
3. Tracking & Status Management
To track the status of real-time audio interaction sessions.
To track the status of room lifecycles from creation to closure.
To track the status of participant states (e.g., Muted, Hand-raised).
To track the status of user wallets and financial transaction integrity.
4. Reporting & Analytics
To report on user identity management and access control.
To report on room utilization and lifecycle trends.
To report on call session connectivity success/failure rates.
To report on participant engagement and interaction metrics.
To report on platform safety and moderation accountability.
To report on virtual economy performance and financial auditing.
To report on system events for troubleshooting and operational analysis.
